Detailed Description

Cheax function or macro lambda expression.

Functions are created with the (fn args body) built-in, and macros with the (macro args body) built-in. The two types of lambdas differ in whether their argument list is evaluated or not when the lambda is invoked. For functions (most common), all arguments are pre-evaluated, for macros they are not.

Lambdas cannot be constructed by the C API.
